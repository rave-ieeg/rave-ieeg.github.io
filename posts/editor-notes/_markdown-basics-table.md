#### Markdown Syntax

``` markdown
| Right | Left | Default | Center |
|------:|:-----|---------|:------:|
|   12  |  12  |    12   |    12  |
|  123  |  123 |   123   |   123  |
|    1  |    1 |     1   |     1  |
```

#### Output

| Right | Left | Default | Center |
|------:|:-----|---------|:------:|
|    12 | 12   | 12      |   12   |
|   123 | 123  | 123     |  123   |
|     1 | 1    | 1       |   1    |

Learn more in the article on [Tables](https://quarto.org/docs/authoring/tables.html).
